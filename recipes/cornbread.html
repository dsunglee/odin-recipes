Description

This corn bread recipe is made with real corn meal and honey butter. Add pickled jalapenos and cheddar cheese for an additional kick!

Ingredients

  • Corn Meal
  • Heavy Cream
  • Canned Corn

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl mix corn meal, water, two eggs, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Add pickled jalapeno, cheddar cheese, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
