Clam Chowder

Description

This clam chowder recipe is hearty and delicious. Made with bacon, potatoes, leeks, and real clams, this soup is the perfect comfort food in the Winter.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Clams with juice
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Render bacon.
  2. Set bacon aside.
  3. Saute onions and celery in bacon fat.
  4. Add leeks, potatoes, clams and clam juice.
  5. Add chicken broth and heavy cream.
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
