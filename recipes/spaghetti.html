Description

This classic Italian dish is mouth-watering and slightly spicy. Featuring basil and a spicy meat sauce, this spaghetti won't last long!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meatballs
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta.
  3. Sweat onions and garlic in a heavy bottom pot.
  4. Add meatballs.
  5. Season to taste.
